HL-OutPaint: High-Resolution Long-Range Video Outpainting with a Global Coarse Scaffold

(SIGGRAPH 2026)

Jeongeun Park · Janghyeok Han · Geonung Kim · Hyun-Seung Lee · Kyuha Choi · Youngseok Han · Sunghyun Cho

POSTECH · Samsung Electronics, Visual Display Business

Abstract

We present HL-OutPaint, a novel framework for high-resolution long-range video outpainting. Our method introduces a global coarse scaffold that enables consistent and coherent generation across extended spatial regions and long temporal sequences. By combining global structural guidance with high-resolution refinement, HL-OutPaint achieves superior visual quality and temporal consistency compared to prior approaches.

Method

HL-OutPaint decomposes the outpainting task into two stages: (1) global scaffold generation and (2) high-resolution refinement. The global scaffold captures long-range dependencies and ensures structural consistency, while the refinement stage enhances local details and visual fidelity.

Results

Our approach demonstrates strong performance on challenging video outpainting benchmarks, producing high-quality results with improved spatial coherence and temporal stability.
